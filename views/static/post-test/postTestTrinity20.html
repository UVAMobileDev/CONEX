The Post - Test

The Post Test is a great way to see how much you have improved over the course of QBIT. It is also what we will use for your mathematics placement here at Trinity! Note this is not the same as a knowledge check that some of you have seen as you reach the end of ALEKS. In fact, to take the post test, you need to go to a brand-new ALEKS course that we've set up just for this purpose.

Here's what you need to do:

Follow the link to the PLACE HOLDER FOR POST-TEST. This will take you to "QBIT 2020 Post-Test" in ALEKS.

You will be asked to take an Initial Knowledge Check. This is the Post-Test. When you have finished the Initial Knowledge Check, you will be able to see your score. Please do not continue on to "Start my Path".

Please complete the Post-Test by Midnight, Sunday, July 26th . Beginning the following week, Dr. Tunstall will follow up with information concerning potential placement changes based on scores.
